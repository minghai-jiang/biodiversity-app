Our service

Ellipsis digitizes and automates inventory taking and monitoring for productive and responsible forestry and forest resource management.

We provide insights on changes in forest cover and forest conditions for performance, compliance, and risk assessments. Helping you minimize manual data analysis and field checkups by delivering relevant statistics to all of your digital devices in near real time. In the field or in the office, anywhere on earth.

  • We take inventory of forest cover and track logging activity and other forest disturbances.
  • We track growth, health, and stress trends for forests, such as biomass, drought, chlorophyll content, and snow cover.
  • We aggregate insights to meaningful levels such as permit areas, reserves, concessions, or annual cutting areas so you are always up to date on the status of your resources.

The information is deposited on a self-updating database and map viewer that you can connect to in the way that fits you best, such as through an alert service or with monthly statistics reports. How you choose to integrate is completely flexible. We are always on standby to advise!
