Our service

Ellipsis digitizes and automates inventory taking and monitoring of land cover, water dynamics, landscape elements, and landscape conditions.

By providing insights on the status of, and changes in, landscape conditions and dynamics, automatically and in near real time, we generate cost-effective geo-statistics and geo-information for compliance management, risk assessments, policy making, planning, and (land, water, and coastal) management.

By helping you minimize time-consuming and costly data analysis and field visits, you save monitoring cost while gaining the comprehensive information that you need to allocate resources effectively.

  • We take a detailed inventory of countrywide land cover and land cover changes. Both historic and in near real time.
  • We monitoring for KPIs and (non-)compliance to land use or land management agreements in agriculture, natural resource management, surface water management or otherwise.
  • We aggregate insights to meaningful levels such as for permit areas, municipalities, river catchments, agricultural fields, or nature reserves that may carry management requirements, KPIs, or legal restrictions, making the information highly actionable for you and your partners.

The information (satellite imagery, numerical, and textual data) is deposited on a self-updating database. This database connects to your existing IT infrastructure in a modular way. Via an external API, all the necessary information can run seamlessly in (already existing) information systems, statistical reports, or platforms. How you choose to integrate the provided information is completely flexible and we are always on standby to advice!

Why Ellipsis?

Monitoring at the cutting edge of innovation.

While satellite data has been around for a while and many organizations make use of it, reliable, continuous and low threshold use by non-experts has always lagged behind. In large part, this is due to the inability of providers to completely automate the analysis and delivery process in both a reliable and affordable way.

As a company with an expertise in IT and AI, we use a scalable and modular analysis pipeline to deliver information on a large variety of landscape dynamics and conditions, anywhere on earth, in a uniform way. We are a front runner in leveraging the most recent developments in AI software and cloud computing, running computer vision based analyses that outperform hardcoded models by taking all data patterns into account.

As a company, it is our main focus to make the right insights available like a utility. As easy as water from a faucet. Our methods benefit users in usability, quality, and affordability.
